History

Dr.Sucheta Kore

Continuing the legacy of his father Dr. Anand Kore further expanded this hospital and renamed it as Kore Hospital in 1997.

Also, in 1999 he established Kore Infertility and Test Tube Baby Centre for the first time on Kolhapur district.

In 2003, Dr. Anand along with his wife Dr. Sucheta set-up Galaxy Multi-Specialty hospital at Warananagar with a specific aim of Healing with Feeling.

The main objective was to get medical facilities at reasonable rates to needy people.

Warananagar is a small rural township near Kolhapur. The area is close to the villages in Panhala, Shahuwadi and Hatkalangade talukas of Kolhapur district which are known as remote hilly areas and also some villages in Shirala and Valva talukas of Sangli district.

Since the last 17 years, this hospital has become popular for medical services for patients in all areas.

The hospital has become a major support to the general public as it provides satisfactory services to many patients

Galaxy Hospital is honored with two national awards for its exceptional patient care.
